Soccer

Soccer (or depending on where your from) is a sport where you try to kick the ball in your opponents net, but at the same time, your trying to defend your side of the half.In other words, Association football,is also referred to as football or soccer, is a team sport in which two teams of 11 players compete against one another. The players mostly use their feet to move the ball across a rectangular surface known as a pitch. The goal of the game is to score more goals than the other team by moving the ball past the goal line and into a goal with a rectangular frame that is being defended by the other team. Two 45-minute halves make up the game's customary format, making the entire contest last 90 minutes. It is regarded as the most widely played sport in the world, with an estimated 250 million players participating in more than 200 nations and territories.
